Xiyun's Blog

description

During my internship, I was told by many friends that a personal website would bring help to my job. Thus, I began to construct my website. XIYUN'S BLOG is used to display and show my blogs as well as projects.

demand analyse

After figuring out on my own why I want to design a personal website before I do, I listed the functions of my personal website.
I hope that my personal website has three functions:
1. Show my projects.
2. Post my blogs.
3. Display my resume.
And the following picture shows the structure of my website.

Prototype Flows

When the function of my personal website is fixed, I started to design prototypes and flows of my website. And the structure of my website is straightforward: A sidebar contains four top level categories (home, portfolio, blog, about), which is controlled by a bottom on the webpages. Under each of these are some subpages, such as pages shown articles and projects.

highlights

All website visitors want feedbacks when they are surfing websites, especially when they click a button or move their cursors to a link. A feedback makes them feel that everything is in control. Thus, while designing the website, I emphasized the feedback when visitors have actions.
